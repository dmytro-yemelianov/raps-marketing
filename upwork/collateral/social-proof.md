## How to Collect Testimonials

### After Project Completion

1. **Request on Upwork**: Ask client to leave detailed review
2. **Email Follow-up**: Request permission to quote them
3. **Specific Questions to Ask**:
   - What was the challenge before working together?
   - What specific results did you achieve?
   - Would you recommend me to others?
   - What stood out about working together?

### Testimonial Request Template

```
Hi [Client Name],

Thank you for the successful project! If you have a moment, I'd really appreciate a brief testimonial I could use in my portfolio. 

A few questions that might help:
- What challenge were you facing before we worked together?
- What results did the project deliver?
- What stood out about working with me?

Feel free to keep it short - even 2-3 sentences would be valuable.

Also, would you be open to me using your name/company, or would you prefer to remain anonymous?

Thanks again!
[Your Name]
```
