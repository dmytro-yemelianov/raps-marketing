## Application Strategy

### Response Time Matters

| Response Window | Priority |
|-----------------|----------|
| < 1 hour | Highest chance of being seen |
| 1-4 hours | Good chance |
| 4-24 hours | Acceptable for good fits |
| > 24 hours | Lower priority in client's view |

### Proposal Customization Checklist

Before submitting:
- [ ] Read entire job post carefully
- [ ] Reference specific requirements they mentioned
- [ ] Explain relevant experience
- [ ] Propose clear next step (call, questions)
- [ ] Keep concise (don't overwhelm)
- [ ] Use appropriate proposal template
- [ ] Attach relevant portfolio item

### Connects Strategy

**When to spend more connects:**
- Exact match for APS/Forge work
- Enterprise clients with large budgets
- Long-term project potential
- Boosted visibility worth the investment

**When to conserve connects:**
- Many competing applicants already
- Vague requirements
- Price-focused client language
- Quick turnaround expected
